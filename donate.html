Support the work

The foundation is built to be free at the point of access.
Your contribution is how that stays true.

AI literacy training, AI tool reviews, public guides, and policy frameworks are delivered free to the people and organizations the foundation serves. Donations, grants, and partnerships are how that work gets done.

What your contribution funds

Concrete work, not overhead.

  1. AI literacy training and coaching, delivered free

    Practical AI literacy sessions and one-to-one coaching for Danish nonprofits, schools, clinics, public-sector teams, small business owners, and the leaders of small mission-driven organizations. Free at the point of access. Designed to support Article 4 compliance under the EU AI Act.

  2. Independent AI tool reviews

    Free reviews of AI tools for small organizations, covering privacy, bias, safety, dignity, and operational fit, for organizations that cannot afford a commercial AI ethics review.

  3. Organizational transformation and policy work

    AI adoption roadmaps, governance frameworks, and policy templates for small and medium-sized enterprises, nonprofits, foundations, and public-sector institutions that do not have in-house AI-policy capacity.

  4. Public guides in English and Danish

    Plain-language guides on AI literacy, responsible deployment, and AI governance, published as open public goods.

  5. The infrastructure that keeps the foundation honest

    Bookkeeping, registration, audit, and the basic operating costs that keep the foundation accountable to its bylaws and to the public it serves.

Our commitment to donors

Honesty about money.

The foundation publishes annual public accounts. We will not accept funds from organizations whose AI use we have publicly criticized. We will not accept funds that compromise our independence, our ethical limits, or our public-benefit purpose.

If you have any question about how a donation will be used, write to us. We will answer honestly.
